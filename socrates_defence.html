Socrates

About Socrates

  1. 470-399 BC
  2. Greek philosopher from Athens.
  3. One of the three greatest figures of the ancient period of Western philosophy
  4. He was admired by his followers for his self-mastery
  5. He was the first Greek philosopher to seriously explore questions of ethics
  6. His style of philosophizing was to engage in public conversations about some human excellence
  7. Socrates was widely hated in Athens, because he regularly embarrassed people by making rhem appear ignorant and foolish
  8. After being convicted, he could have proposed a resonable penalty short of death but initially refused
